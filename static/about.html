About the Business

Welcome to Sangeeta Patwa's Maharashtrian Jewelry Collection!

At our store, we celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Maharashtra through our exquisite jewelry pieces. Our collection features a blend of traditional and contemporary designs that cater to every taste and occasion.

We take pride in our handcrafted jewelry, made with the finest materials and utmost attention to detail. Whether you are looking for classic pieces like the Nath, Mundawadi, or Mangalsutra, or modern designs with a traditional touch, we have something for everyone.

Our mission is to bring the timeless beauty of Maharashtrian jewelry to a wider audience, preserving its elegance and cultural significance.

If you are passionate about traditional jewelry and want to explore our unique collection, please visit our store or browse through our online catalog.

Stay connected with us to discover new designs and exclusive offers. Thank you for being a part of our journey in celebrating Maharashtrian heritage through jewelry.

Explore our collection and embrace the beauty of tradition with a modern twist.

Follow us on social media and stay updated with our latest creations and special offers.
